Three data streams that converge in the dashboard.

LunchBox network synthetic_timeseries.json
10 sensors × 10 timesteps → sensor ID, lat/lng, count, active flag. Powers: time slider, sensor pins, heatmap intensity, trend chart, KPI cards.
Drone feature layers feature_layers.geojson.json
3 toggleable layers: standing water (5 polygons), vegetation density (3 polygons), container risk (8 points). Powers: LayerControl toggles, map overlay popups.
Intervention + placement intervention_and_placement.json
Fogging zone polygon (visible step 6+) + 2 recommended next-placement pins (visible step 10). Powers: "Mark intervention" button, gold pin, reduction metric.
